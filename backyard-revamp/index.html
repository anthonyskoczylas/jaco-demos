Where the jungle meets the break

The Backyard Hotel sits on the lush central Pacific coast in Playa Hermosa, 4 km south of Jacó, one of Costa Rica's best-known surfing and sunbathing beaches.

Your ocean-front room overlooks the Refugio de Vida Silvestre national park, with breathtaking whitewater views of the Pacific. Wake up, walk down, paddle out.
