Workcations in Costa Rica

A calm place to focus — and a great excuse not to.

The Coconut Office was built for people who work remotely and live fully. Air-conditioned offices, fast fiber, a pool in the middle of the property, and a yoga deck with a glimpse of the sea. Whenever the power drops in Playa Hermosa, our generator keeps the AC, the Wi-Fi and the coffee machine running without a flicker.

It's an adults-only retreat designed for deep work and easy living — familiar faces, a relaxed atmosphere, and that warm, village-like charm that makes Playa Hermosa feel like home.

Location

Calle Hermosa, Playa Hermosa.

Just past Jacó — about five minutes by car — on a quiet street 300 metres from the coastal road. A white, three-storey building on the left with a wooden gate, one of the first past the first bend.

Getting here

Pass the town of Playa Hermosa, then about 400 metres on, the first entrance on the left is Calle Hermosa. The Coconut Office sits 300 metres in — easy to spot, just past the first bend.
