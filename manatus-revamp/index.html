Named for the Lady of the Sea

Tortuguero National Park is one of the most extraordinary places on Earth, reachable only by boat or plane on Costa Rica's northern Caribbean coast. Just getting here is part of the magic.

Manatus takes its name from the manatee, the shy "Lady of the Sea" that hides in the warm, marshy channels of the jungle. Come find the wild, and let us take care of the rest.
