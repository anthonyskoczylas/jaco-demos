Welcome to paradise

A sanctuary between the waves and the jungle

On Costa Rica's Pacific coast, between the constant sound of the waves and the whisper of the jungle, there's a place where time stands still. With more than two decades of history and direct beach access, Terraza del Pacífico celebrates the real beauty of Playa Hermosa.

Here, luxury isn't measured in gold — but in surf mornings, sunsets that paint the sky, and nights that end with your feet in the sand.
