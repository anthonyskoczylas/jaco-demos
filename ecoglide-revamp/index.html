On the slopes of the volcano

Ecoglide Arenal Park sits among rivers, forests, mountains and gardens on the flank of the Arenal Volcano, with direct contact with the flora and fauna of Costa Rica's tropical rainforest.

La Fortuna is the home of adventure. We put it all in one park, safely, for the whole family.
