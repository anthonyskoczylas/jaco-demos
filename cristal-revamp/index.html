Where the whales come to the coast

Between the ocean and the mountains

Cristal Ballena sits on a hill near Uvita and Marino Ballena National Park, embedded between the Pacific Ocean and rainforest-covered mountains. The position gives you breathtaking ocean views while a cooling breeze drifts down from the peaks.

Swim in the large ocean-view pool, dine at the gourmet restaurant, wander the manicured gardens, and watch a sunset you'll carry with you.
