The lodge

Small, quiet, and right on the sand

Tortuga del Mar is a handful of ocean-view rooms and studios wrapped around a pool in a tropical garden, opening straight onto one of Costa Rica's finest surf beaches. No crowds, no resort buzz — just the waves, the garden, and the kind of quiet you came here for.

Run with care by hosts who live on-site, it's the kind of place guests come back to year after year.
